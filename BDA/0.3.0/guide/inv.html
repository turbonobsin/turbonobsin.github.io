Inventories


Inventories:

  • Press "R" to open/close personal inventory. This is where you can use items you've collected to craft other items and also see information about items. This is the ITEM crafting menu.
  • Press "F" to bring up/close the right click action menu at your current mouse position. The only option at the moment is craft here.

    Craft Here:

    • This brings up the OBJECT crafting window. Choose an object to build and once you hit build in the top right, a preview location is displayed where the object will be built. This preview can be moved here by using the Arrow Keys.

Small Inventory / Quick Inventory

  • This is located in the bottom left, just to the right of the player stats HUD
  • Open/Close quick inventory with "Enter"
  • Cycle through tabs catagories with Left and Right arrow, or "A" and "D" with Swordman Class
