Status Effects

Frozen:

  • Causes the enemy process, move, and animate slower.
  • Lasts for multiple seconds, this time is shorted when the enemy takes fire element damage.

Stunned: (not universal to all enemies, currently only supported on Speed Roller)

  • Enemy is completely stopped of all actions and is fully open to attack.
  • Lasts for a few seconds.
  • Yellow sparks flash by the enemy.

Wet/Drenched (planned but not implimented)

  • Makes the enemy resistance to fire element damage.
  • Makes the enemy much more vulnerable to ice element damage.
  • The likelihood of stronger ice techniques (Ice Prison, not implimented) to be successful
