Shovel


The purpose of this tool is to dig dirt in rows and channels in order to have water flow to crops from a source point. (having items move down water channels is planned)

Currently only water can move in the channels it doesn't affect the crops in any way.

Same controls as Hoe and Tamper
Hold right click and let go on whatever position you want it at to create a starting point.

  • From any piece of tilled dirt, hover over it until a white indicator pixels shows
  • Hold down the left mouse button, and move the player in the direction you want to extend the row
  • Don't move the mouse while extending the row unless you know what you are doing because it could be confusing.

Tilled dirt is a lighter brown and dug dirt is a darker brown.
