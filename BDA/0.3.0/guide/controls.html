Controls


Pausing:

  • Press "Escape" to Pause/Unpause

Movement:

  • Movement is usually done with WASD but while using the katana weapon flips the WASD and arrow keys actions for more combat move diversity.
  • Spacebar to jump.

Small Inventory / Quick Inventory

  • This is located in the bottom left, just to the right of the player stats HUD
  • Open/Close quick inventory with "Enter"
  • Cycle through tabs catagories with Left and Right arrow, or "A" and "D" with Swordman Class

Using the "(None)" weapon/tool

  • This is your tool for interacting with the environment.
  • It does not do damage.
  • Left click to swing in direction of cursor.
  • Can break small objects such as sticks and rocks.
