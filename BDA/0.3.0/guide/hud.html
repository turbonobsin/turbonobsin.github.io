HUD


Player Stats:

  • HP: Health Points
  • EP: Energy Points
  • The number on the left represents how much you have and then the hp/number represents the max.
  • Max HP and EP is increased based on LVL.

  • While you aren't attacking, EP regens by 2 per second, but if you are standing still it regens by 6 per second.

  • In the top left of the player stats hud is class name.
  • LVL of your current class is in the top right and the gray bar underneath represents how close you are to the next level.

  • When you have over maximum HP and EP you are a MEGA...
