WAND Controls

Attacking:

  • Aim with mouse and hold left mouse button for a bit to charge up spell, once the white indicator at your mouse position is gone, it will display a guide of where the attack will happen and then let go to execute spell.

(With the quick inventory closed)

Spell Menu:
  • press right or left arrow to bring up the spell menu and use the arrow keys to cycle through spells. Press "Enter" to close.
  • press up or down arrow to cycle through spell element types.

Aiming / Locking on (Needs to be more intuitive in the future)

  • Simply right clicking to lock on is the fastest way but you can't choose which hitbox, so if there was a weak point on the enemy use the "Q" method to specifically lock on to the head for example.
  • (without holding "Q") RIGHT CLICK to select the first available hitbox on an enemy. RIGHT CLICK again to deselect.
  • Hold "Q" to display the available targets that you can lock on to. (Targets will only appear if in a certain range of your mouse pointer)
    • *Targets are displayed at the center of hitboxes*
  • While a target is displayed, LEFT CLICK on it to select it. Right click without holding "Q" to deselect.
