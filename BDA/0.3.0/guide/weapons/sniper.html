SNIPER Controls

*This weapon is not fully completed*

Differences from Pistol

  • The normal shooting speed is much slower
  • You can't move while shooting
  • Damage per bullet is greater but slower speed, more important to hit the target everytime with this weapon to maximize damage.
  • Pushed backward slightly from the shot (Planned but not implimented)

Attacking: (same as pistol)

  • Hold left mouse button to shoot with normal attack, does not take energy.
  • Hold "Shift" while shooting for a 2x faster shot that takes 1 energy per shot.
  • Hold "C" while shooting for a 4x faster shot and 2x faster bullet that takes 4 energy per shot.

Aiming:

  • Aiming is unfinished with this weapon and it acts the same way as every other weapon.
  • As of this time, the bullet does not go to where the target is.
