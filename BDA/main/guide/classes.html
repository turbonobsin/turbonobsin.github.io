Classes

Swordman:

  • A class for close range attacks focused on dodging and using the right move at the right time.

  • Equippable Weapons: (these lists will change when more weapons are added)
    • Katana
    • Knife

Gunman:

  • A class for long range attacks focused on doing consistant damage to enemies.

  • Equippable Weapons:
    • Pistol
    • Sniper
    • Knife

Mage:

  • A class for combat magic use. Used for attacks of all kinds and stunning or freezing the enemy. Some defense capabilities.

  • Equippable Weapons:
    • Wand
    • Knife

Sage:

  • A class for support magic use. Used for healing, bufs, and superior defense such as making barriers and walls.

  • Equippable Weapons:
    • Staff
    • Knife

Necroman:

  • A class focused on dark magic and bringing the dead back to life and using the enemy against themselves.

  • Equippable Weapons:
    • Knife

Bugman:

  • A class for mobbing, tracking fast moving enemies, and doing "damage over time" attacks by manipulating bugs.

  • Equippable Weapons:
    • Knife
