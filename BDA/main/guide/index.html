*On a chromebook:*
  • Left click is a touchpad click with 1 finger
  • Right click is a touchpad click with 2 fingers
  • Middle click or clicking scroll wheel is a touchpad click with 3 fingers

Lag: There is a lot of lag when the game first starts but once you get moving and doing stuff it runs at 60 fps smooth even during rain on the chromebook.
