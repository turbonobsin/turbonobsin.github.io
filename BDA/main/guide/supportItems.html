Fragrance Bottles

  • Different smells can be created by using different ingredients.
  • Some smells attract or repel certain animals and some attract or repel certain enemies.
  • When luring an animal you need, make sure you know what enemy it may also lure because it may lead to a massive group of enemies coming after you.
