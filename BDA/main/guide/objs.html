Objects


Stick

  • Sticks lie on the ground and simple are there to be broken to collect sticks.

Small Rock

  • Small rocks are there to be broken to collect small rocks.

Water Well

  • Contains 100 buckets of water capacity
  • Water can be extracted/inserted using a bucket

Medium Barrel

  • Can hold 4 buckets of any fluid
  • Fluid can be extracted/inserted using a bucket
  • If there is dug dirt beneath the spout (one black pixel on the left side of the barrel, one day you will be able to flip this) then if you hit the barrel with (NONE) then it will spit out a bucket of fluid into the dug dirt
  • The capcity visually chances based on its capacity

Tree

  • Trees are procedurally and randomly generated
  • Can be cut down with an axe to get wood
  • Currently there is only one type of tree, there will be many more types later

House!

  • In future versions you will be able to create your own house and fully customize the exterior via blueprints and add any objects to the inside. (This feature has already been started in the PC Version)
  • You can go inside houses
  • The inside of houses are separate from the real world but you can still fight and place objects inside

Campfire

  • Currently spawns prelit
  • Will be used to make light and cook food but that is for the future

Cooking Stand (Spit)

  • Currently no functionality
  • Goes hand in hand with Campfire
  • Early game way to cook food

Cave Entrances (Only in PC Version)

  • Allow you to go underground into the dark caves to find treasure and metals

Decoration:

Boulders, Wild/Tall grass (light and dark color), Tents

Wild/Tall Grass

  • In the PC Version tall grass changes colors based on what biome its in
