Mutant Wolf

    • Type: Mammal

    Weak to:

    • Fire (2x damage)

    Resistant against:

    • None

    Moves:

    • Jumps in a zigzag pattern to reach target
    • Once in combat range, the wolf will circle you waiting to strike.
    • When it decides to strike, it will lunge forward with massive open jaws and then retreat backward to either continue circling or zigzag after you again.
