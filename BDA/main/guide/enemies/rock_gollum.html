Rock Gollum

    • Type: Natural

    Weak to:

    • None

    Resistant against:

    • None

    Moves:

    • If you are in his sight but not very close, the gollum will pick up a brown rock from the ground and through it at you. The rock will do damage and it knocks you back.
    • If you are extremely close he will slowly try to walk away from you.
    • If you are fairly close he will slowly walk toward you.
    • Once he is in melee range he will swing his fist and punch at you, will causes a lot of damage and knocks you fairly far.
