Speed Roller

    • Type: Machine

    Weak to:

    • Lightning (2x damage)

    Resistant against:

    • None

    Moves:

    • The Speed Roller will find you and move in bursts until it gets to you.
    • If the roller is faily close but not super close, he will circle you in bursts.
    • Once he gets in combat range, he may charge forward with spinning blades that will do damage, or he may do a fast cyclone swirl with blades out.
    • If he is very close to you he will do a stomping attack that does a massive amount of damage.
    • While moving or attacking, there is a chance that it decides not to put blades out or it may decide to jump.

    • When hitting the speed roller, there is a chance that you will stun it.

    • *This enemy is really fun to fight!*
