Spells

*Note: Only the spells listed in this doc are actually implimented, all the other spells listed in the actual spell menu are planned but not finished.*

Fire:

  • Shoots a fireball in given direction and height.
  • Fireball does not penetrate, once it hits an enemy it is destroyed.
  • On impact the fireball explodes causing slight area damage.

Ice:

  • Shoots a sliding piece of ice in a given direction that always moves along the ground.
  • Sliding ice penetrates and will give the FROZEN status effect toward any enemy or animal it touches.

Earth:

  • Causes plates in the ground to shoot up in a given direction that always moves along the ground.
  • Rising Earth penetrates and will do Earth element damage on hit.

Lightning:

  • Lightning can be spawned anywhere within a 70 pixel radius.
  • Lightning causes a few hits in one spot and does element damage.

Life:

  • Creates a healing aura that will heal 2 incriments over a second. (currently 2 times it will give you 4hp)
  • The amount you are healed by is display with a green number.

Wind:

  • Wind particles spawn around and swirl around the player.
  • Damage is toned down because many hits occur.
  • Good for mobbing.

Energy:

  • Allows you to move really fast.
  • Not a charge up spell, it drains EP during use.

    • Vortex

    • Takes light out of the current location.
    • Sucks in enemies in radius and pulls the screen pixels themselves into it.

Light: (Not final implimentation)

  • Creates light at the current location
  • Sucks in enemys in radius and pulls the screen pixels themselves into it.
