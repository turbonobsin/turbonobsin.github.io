Enemies

Hitboxes

  • Enemies can have 1 or more hitboxes.
  • Holding "Q" will show lock on targets which are displayed at the center of each hitbox.
  • Some hitboxes are weaker and take more damage than other parts.

Weakness

  • Some enemies are weak to certain elements, such as machines are weak to lightning, and mammals are weak to fire.
  • When damage with an element type is delt to an enemy, if that enemy is weak to that element the damage is multiplied by a certain value.
  • Damage amplified by weakness appears as a blue number instead of white

Resistance

  • Resistance is the opposite of weakness, damage is decreased by a certain amount if the enemy is resistant to that element.
  • Damage reduced by resistance will appear as a light orange, yellow number instead of white
  • *Resistance has not been implimented yet*
