PISTOL Controls

Attacking:

  • Hold left mouse button to shoot with normal attack, does not take energy.
  • Hold "Shift" while shooting for a 2x faster shot that takes 1 energy per shot.
  • Hold "C" while shooting for a 4x faster shot and 2x faster bullet that takes 4 energy per shot.

Aiming:

  • Simply right clicking to lock on is the fastest way but you can't choose which hitbox, so if there was a weak point on the enemy use the "Q" method to specifically lock on to the head for example.
  • Upon normal attack, it will aim at selected hitbox.
  • (without "Q" held) RIGHT CLICK on an enemy to automatically lock on the first hitbox on the enemy. Right click again to deselect.
  • Holding "Q" will show available hitboxes in range and you can click with LEFT mouse button to select or click anywhere else to deselect the hitbox.
  • Holding "Q" and RIGHT CLICK, it will show available hitboxes in range, ones hovered over will turn red, and when mouse let go will shoot bullets to those places.
