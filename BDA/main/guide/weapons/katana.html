KATANA Controls

Attacking:

  • Normal Attack

    • Keybind: "D"
    • This is a normal slash in front of you that does not take energy.
    • Holding "D" or pressing "D" right after a move is over will take it to the next move in the sequence. If you wait too long the cycle starts over.
    • The first move is a slash down, second is a slash up, and the third is a more powerful hit that slashes from the front to back and can hit behind you.

  • Energy Attacks (takes EP)

    • Can be chained with normal attacks.
    • For example: Doing 2 swirls in a row and immidiately pressing "D" after the last will cause that normal attack to be the 3rd stronger attack in the sequence.

    • Swirl: keybind "S"
      • Hits all enemies around you
      • Good for mobbing
