Bucket


The bucket holds fluids and lets you move fluids between objects manually (only water so far is implimented)

Controls

  • Hover mouse over a place that can hold water while you are in range of the object. It will display a white rectangle to show you where liquid is stored and show (current capacity / max capacity) in buckets
  • If you have an empty bucket, RIGHT CLICK to take fluid from that spot and put it in the bucket. In your quick inventory beside the word bucket "WATER" in blue will show saying you have a bucket filled with water.
  • With fluid in the bucket, you can LEFT CLICK on a fluid storage spot to put it there.
  • You can also put fluid down in dug dirt.
