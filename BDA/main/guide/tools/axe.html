Axe


An axe is used to cut down trees and cut up the fallen branches to get wood.

Controls:

  • BEWARE! When a tree slams against the ground it can do major damage to you or destroy crops, tilled dirt, or objects or even knock other trees down!
  • Get close to the base of a tree and when you see the white pixel indicator on the base of the tree, Left Click repeatedly (will be slowed down in the future, this is just for testing purposes).
  • To break the branches, simple Left click multiple times on a fallen branch and it will break.
